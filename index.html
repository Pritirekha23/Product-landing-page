Frequenly Asked Questions

Where are you based?

We are located in the UK and hold all our stock in our own premises just outside Bedford in the South East of England. We only sell items that we have in stock, so there is no long wait times for stock to arrive before we fulfil your order.

How Quickly are orders dispatched?

All orders placed before 4 pm (Mon-Fri) are sent out the same day. The delivery speed will depend a little on your delivery option but is usually 1-3 working days.

Do you offer Next Day Delivery?

Yes, we offer Next Day delivery on all Mainland UK orders. Simply select the option at checkout. The exact cost will vary depending on your order, but will be around £6.50. Orders over £100 qualify for free Next Day delivery. Only valid for orders placed before 4pm, Monday-Friday.

Do you offer free Delivery?

Yes, we offer free standard delivery on all UK and EU orders. All UK orders over £50 qualify for free Signed for 1st class delivery. We use Royal Mail for our deliveries. You will be able to select your preferred delivery method at checkout. For more information, see our delivery information page

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we can gift wrap any products, which is perfect to save you time or send it straight to a loved one for a special occasion. You can include a gift note which will be handwritten and attached to the wrapping. You can find out more and add gift wrapping here.

I am purchasing from the EU, will I have to pay any import fees,VAT or duties?

No, we are IOSS registered, so we will capture all taxes for orders to EU countries during your purchase and pass this information on to the carrier automatically. You will not need to pay any extra fees or taxes upon receipt of your order. Taxes are included in our pricing, so the price you see on our website will be the price you pay.

Do you offer any warranty?

Yes, all the wallets we sell come with a 2-year manufacturer warranty that covers any faults or issues outside of normal wear and tear. Many of our wallets come with an extended warranty if you register your wallet.
